Bead Crochet Design

This is a beta version of a Bead Crochet Design Tool. It applies ideas from the design framework described in the book Crafting Conundrums: Puzzles and Patterns for the Bead Crochet Artist by Ellie Baker and Susan Goldstine, and was created as a supplement to the book. Use at your own risk, since it is still in development, but stay tuned for more info and instructions soon!
