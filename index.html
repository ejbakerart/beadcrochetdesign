About Crafting Conundrums

This is a beta version of a Bead Crochet Design Tool. It applies ideas from the design framework described in the book Crafting Conundrums: Puzzles and Patterns for the Bead Crochet Artist by Ellie Baker and Susan Goldstine. It was created as a supplement to an upcoming new edition of the book. The software was developed by Ellie Baker and Scott Vorthmann.

Use it at your own risk, since it is still in development, but stay tuned for more info and instructions soon. Feedback may be sent to craftingconundrums@gmail.com.
